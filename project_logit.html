Interpretation

Portfolio managers from a telecommunications company asked me to help them understand which factors contribute to customer churn (customers leaving to competing companies) and also to predict which customers are most likely to churn. According to the results, clients with the highest churn rate are

  • new clients,
  • clients paying month-to-month (without longer subscription plan),
  • clients paying via electronic check,
  • clients having internet among services.

The takeaway message:

  • The company is good at recruiting new clients but struggle to keep them.
  • The internet service is not competitive.
Using electronic check and month-to-month plan is probably not the reason for a client to leave but rather the fact that the client wants to leave is the reason for them to use month-to-month plan and electronic check.
